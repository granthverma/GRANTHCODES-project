About Me.

Hi there! My name is Himanshu verma, and I'm passionate about technology. After spending 4 years as a technical support engineer at Apple, I realized that I love everything about technology - from the way it works to the way it can be used to solve complex problems.
But it wasn't just the technical aspects of the job that I enjoyed - I also loved the sense of satisfaction that came from helping customers solve their problems and making their lives easier. This inspired me to explore other areas of technology and find ways to use my skills and knowledge to make a difference in the world.

But no matter where my career takes me, my passion for technology remains the same. I'm always looking for new ways to use technology to solve problems, make people's lives easier, and make the world a better place.
